About Me

I have explored many cities, but San Diego stole my heart. During my long time residency in the Bay Area, I had the opportunity to develop an alignment of passions in dance, body politics, behavioral sciences, and non-verbal communication. After completing an MFA in Performance Studies, my search for the right community to utilize and further develop these skills led me to Community Interface Services, where I began advocating for disabiltiy rights and services. Supporting participants through the pandemic, remotely, showed me just how criucial information access is. This fueled me to begin a career in web development and join the movement in web inclusion and accessibility.
